Welcome to Glam Sphere, where style meets passion.

OUR STORY

Fashion is about passion, not just clothes

Founded in 2024 by Munendra Gangwar & Archita Singh, Glam Sphere was born out of desire to create a more inclusive and equitable fashion landscape.Frustrated by the lack of opportunities available to emerging designers, we set out to build a platform that celebrates creativity, fosters innovation, and supports the next generation of fashion pioneers. Our website has evolved into a destination for fashion enthusiasts seeking curated collections, unique designs, and a community that shares their love for style.

OUR MISSION

We believe that creativity knows no bounds and that every designer deserves a chance to shine. Our mission is simple: to provide a platform where emerging and underrepresented fashion designers can showcase their talent, reach a wider audience, and thrive in an industry that often overlooks their potential. We're here to challenge the status quo, break down barriers, and champion diversity and inclusivity in fashion.
